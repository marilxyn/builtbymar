ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm

marilyn

My passion for design is rooted in the enjoyment of bringing ideas to life in visually engaging and functional ways. From a young age, creativity has been a core part of who I am, and creating art became the perfect outlet to channel that creativity.

I enjoy helping clients express their brand and vision, and guiding them to discover an aesthetic that feels authentic and impactful. Design, to me, is about crafting solutions that resonate emotionally and provide real, functional value.

Based in the Pacific Northwest — fluent in Figma, HTML/CSS/JS & the full Adobe Creative Suite.

What drives me

MY MISSION

To shape innovative and impactful digital experiences that merge visual appeal with purpose and functionality. Through graphic design, UX design, and web development, I aim to foster meaningful connections between brands and their audiences — prioritizing a user-centered approach that elevates every interaction.

What I stand for

MY VALUES

01 COMMUNICATION Essential when working with clients — understanding needs clearly leads to work that actually lands.
02 ADAPTABILITY Tech moves fast and trends shift. Staying flexible keeps the work relevant and the process smooth.
03 CREATIVITY The driving force behind original, engaging design that makes people stop and look twice.
04 INNOVATION Challenging conventional ideas to push digital products somewhere unexpected and memorable.
05 FUNCTIONALITY Beautiful work that doesn't perform is just decoration. Every design decision has a purpose.
