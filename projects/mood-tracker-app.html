Mood Tracker App Prototype

Overview

I designed a mood tracker app prototype in Figma. Titled Moodscapes, the app helps users monitor and reflect on their emotional well-being. The app features a dynamic dashboard with statistical sections that visualize mood trends over time, along with a unique "Mood Map" calendar that provides a color-coded overview of daily moods. Users can log their emotional state through the mood prompt function by selecting a mood and optionally adding a personal note. These notes are also visible within the Mood Map, offering additional context to each day's mood entry. There is also a profile section available. This prototype emphasizes clarity, usability, and emotional insight through thoughtful UX/UI design.
